Drug addiction

emoji_objects Addiction refers to the harmful need to consume substances that have damaging consequences on the user.Addiction affects not just the body but also on the person’s mental health and soundness of mind. Addiction is one of the most severe health problems faced around the world and is termed as a chronic disease. A widespread disorder ranges from drugs, alcohol addiction to gambling, and even phone addiction.

done_all One of the most unfortunate yet common addictions that affect millions today is drug addiction. Also referred to as substance – use disorder, it is the addiction to substances that harm neurological functioning and a person’s behavior. The essay provides relevant information on this topic.

  1. Drug addiction refers to taking harmful substances that affect a person’s brain functions and behavior. It involves taking legal and illegal drugs, and the person is unable to stop using them. It is also referred to as substance- use disorders
  2. Harmful drugs include alcohol, cocaine, heroin, opioids, painkillers, nicotine, etc.
  3. The harmful drugs cause an excessive release of dopamine or the happy hormone, which causes the person to take more.

Reasons of drug addiction

  • Genetics
  • social environment
  • age of first use
  • mental illness
  • early childhood trauma
  • adult trauma
